W & W GRAINS CORP.

W & W Grains Corp. is involved in international trade and commerce for more than two decades. It has, over the years, been drawing closer on a global basis producers, traders and consumers and providing services with increasingly high levels of efficiency and reputation. In commodity trading the major items are wheat, rice, sugar, oils, oils seeds, fertilizer and steel. Since liberation W & W Grains Corp. is one of the highest supplier of Agro-based commodities in Bangladesh.

Starch & Sweeteners

W&W Grains started its SNS (Starch & Sweeteners) division with the vision of serving the pharma industry with quality raw material. Since 2005 WW SNS division started indenting as well as supplying many excipients from their own warehouse which they are importing from world renowned suppliers. WW SNS supplying Pharma excipients to some big pharmaceutical companies like Beximco, Square, Incepta , Eskayef etc. We have state of the art warehouse facility to ensure our product quality.
